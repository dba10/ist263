Guinea Bissau

Guinea-Bissau is a small yet beautiful West African country located on the Atlantic coast. 2023 was my first time ever visiting Guinea-Bissau, and honestly, I think it's the most beautiful country I've ever been to. The beaches were stunning, the food was top-tier, and the people were welcoming. This page will be highlighting the beauty of the country. Below are some pictures that I took in the country.
